Renewable & Non Renewable Resources

Renewable Resources

Advantages

  • They get renewed naturally, and hence we will never run out of renewable resources if used wisely.

  • Generally renewable resources have a small carbon footprint, thus making them cleaner forms of energy.

  • They are generally abundant, and hence easily accissible at many places.

Disadvantages

  • Some types of renewable resources require expensive technology to harness.

  • They are not conventional resources.

  • Technology used to harness some renewable resources cause damage to bio-diversity.

Non Renewable Resources

Advantages

  • Generally cheaper and harnessed on large scale as they are conventional resources.

  • Non renewable resources which are fuels have high energy levels (calorific value).

Disadvantages

  • They are limited in nature and take enourmous amounts of time to get renewed naturally.

  • Generally they are not available everywhere and are extracted from few places around the world.

  • They have a bigger carbon footprint and play a significant role in global greenhouse emissions as well as pollution.
